Nikesh Shrestha

Mechanical Engineering | Robotics | Machine Learning | Automotive




Hello World. My name is Nikesh Shrestha. I am a 21-year-old pursuing a career path in Engineering, more specifically, in the specialty areas of Robotics, Automotive and Automation. I am currently getting my Bachelor of Science in Engineering from the University of Maryland, and I am planning to pursue higher education to earn my master’s degree, then a Doctorate in Engineering. Getting a doctorate degree in engineering means that I would be one of the best in this field of study. Ever since being born in Nepal, I always had the hunger of being the best at anything I did. Hence, my desire to go to grad school and become among the best in the field of engineering. Getting a doctorate in Engineering is something in the far future, and my current focus is to set the foundation for my career path. As a senior in college, my immediate goals are to graduate and to get in a good school for my master’s degree in engineering.

As you already know that I have decided to follow a career path in engineering. But why engineering? It wasn’t until my junior year in high school where my interest in engineering had sparked. In my first experience with Intro to Engineering was what led me to follow this career path. I had my first engineering project to build a mouse trap vehicle. I remember enjoying every moment of brainstorming and building the vehicle; also, seeing my ideas come together into one finite object gave me a lot of happiness. My year with Intro to Engineering followed with many more projects which gave me more and more joy each time. In retrospect, my interest in engineering crystalized through the following years as I enrolled in my high school’s introductory engineering course, joined the robotics club and decided to major in engineering.

Currently, my interest lies in engineering, especially Robotics, Automotive and Automation. I am planning to pursue higher education with a concentration in Automation and Automotive. Additionally, my carrer goal lies in working in automotive industry.
