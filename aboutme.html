Research Lab: NJIT Advanced Energy Systems and Microdevices Laboratory

Research Advisor: Dr. Eon Soo Lee (Research site)

Research Mentor: Yudong Wang

Research Topic: Visualization and Characterization of Fluid Drop on a surface-treated PDMS material.

During this summer research experience at NJIT with Dr. Lee in his microdevices laboratory, I studied the characterization of fluid drop on a PDMS material treated with Oxygen Plasma on the surface for implementation in Passive Plasma Separation in a Microchannel. I developed a MATLAB code which semi-automatically determines the wetting angles made by the fluid on the treated PDMS. The wetting angles determine the wettability of the surface depending on variable treatment levels. The ability to control the wettability of the material can be implemented for passively separating plasma from whole blood for disease detection and monitoring.
